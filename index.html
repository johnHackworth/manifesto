The “No Bullshit” Manifesto

We are professional software developers, and we are passionate about it. As such, we strive to improve and are open to learn and apply any methodologies that can help us to do so. We do not, however, buy into silver bullet solutions or feel-good empty talk. We call bullshit, and we mean it.

These will be our principles of bullshit-calling:

We will listen and read. Not everything is bullshit, but if it sounds too good to be true, it’s probably not true. Be open, but critical.

We will call bullshit when we see it. This will make people unhappy, but we value honesty above popularity.

We will admit being wrong when we are. We will be wrong sometimes, and that’s ok. We are learning, too.

Be open, honest and critical: call bullshit and mean it
